Registration for the Guided Cyber Crime Awareness Training

August 17, 2021 / By Collcom2015

"This is a campus/city/state/national level cyber champion ambassador programme. This is an initiative by CollCom to create awareness about Cybercrime, Cybersecurity, Laws and Acts, and Complain Reporting Helpline to all ages of people though Guided CyberCrime Awareness Quiz. Interested students can join this opportunity and become a Cyber Champion role model of our society."

Registration Link: Click here for Registration
Day to day Inter Task:

1. Learn, Study and Understand the different modes of Cyber Crime and Security, and share the Cybercrime Awareness Guided Quiz to maximum People
2. Enroll minimum 100 Participants to become Cyber Champion at College Level, or
3. Enroll Minimum 200 Participants to become Cyber Champion at District or City Level, or
4. Enroll Minimum 300 Participants to become Cyber Champion at State Level, or
5. Enroll Minimum 500 Participants to become Cyber Champion at National Level
6. Follow and Share the knowledge poster of Cyber Crime from our official social media to your Social media network
7. Actively participate and attend all the Webinars organised by Organizations.
*Selected Cyber Ambassador based on fullfillment of above requirements, will be rewarded with gift hamper and award certificate.
In case of query, contact on 9868189955 or write a mail to pr@collcom.org
